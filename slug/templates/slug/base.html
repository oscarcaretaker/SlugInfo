Slugs of Slugterra
Slugterra is an animated action series that takes place in a mysterious, underground world filled with caverns and creatures. This world, called Slugterra, is hidden beneath our own and inhabited by a variety of slugs, each possessing unique powers and abilities. Slugs are small, cute, and colorful creatures that transform into powerful beings when fired at high speed from a blaster. Each slug type has its own unique abilities, known as "slug energy," that can be harnessed during combat. The underground world of Slugterra is filled with diverse landscapes, from dark caverns to lush ecosystems.
